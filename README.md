# 💼 Sales Performance Dashboard

A multi-page, interactive Tableau dashboard offering a full-spectrum analysis of global sales metrics, customer behavior, and historical performance trends. Built to empower retail, strategy, and executive teams with data-driven visibility across KPIs, geography, consumer insights, and forecasting signals.

---

## 📋 Project Overview

The Sales Performance Dashboard spans three distinct views to evaluate the organization's sales strategy from multiple angles:

1. **Global Sales Overview**  
2. **Customer & Order Insights**  
3. **Time Series Forecasting**  

Each page provides its own analytical lens—allowing users to assess profitability, consumer segmentation, operational timing, and trend dynamics across the 2011–2015 period.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Tableau Desktop – Core platform for designing rich visualizations  
• 🧠 Calculated Fields – For aggregating KPIs, discount logic, and YoY calculations  
• 📉 Moving Averages – Applied in time series forecasting  
• 🔁 Dashboard Actions – Enables interactivity across charts and views  
• 📁 File Format – .twb for development and .png for image previews  

---

## 🔍 Data Source

**Source**: Superstore Sales Dataset (Tableau Sample)

Contains detailed order-level records for:  
* Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Discounts  
* Customer segments and regional breakdowns  
* Shipment modes and delivery timelines  
* Monthly and yearly timestamps (2011–2015)  
* Country, category, and product-level insights

## ✨ Features / Highlights

---

### 📄 Page 1: Global Sales Overview  

#### • Business Problem  
Companies struggle to identify geographic performance gaps and product-level profitability in real time across global branches.

#### • Goal  
Visualize global contribution, segment performance, category breakdowns, and overall growth trajectory.

#### • Key Visuals  

* 💰 **Total Sales**: \$12.6M  
* 📈 **Total Profit**: \$1.46M  
* 📉 **Profit Ratio**: 2,433  
* 🧾 **Average Discount**: 14.29%  
* 🌍 Sales & Profit by Country (Map)  
* 📊 Sales by Region and Sub-Category (Bar Chart)  
* 📈 Monthly Sales Growth 2011–2014 (Line Chart)  
* 🥧 Profit Ratio by Segment (Pie Chart)  

---

### 📄 Page 2: Customer & Order Insights  

#### • Business Problem  
Retailers often overlook customer segmentation and order-level efficiency in service delivery and retention strategy.

#### • Goal  
Spot top customers, quantify delivery speed, and correlate discounts with profitability.

#### • Key Visuals  

* 👥 **Unique Customers**: 1,590  
* 📦 **Total Quantity Sold**: 178,312  
* ⏱️ **Average Delivery Time**: 3.97 days  
* 💵 **Sales per Customer**: \$7,951  
* 🔝 Top Customers by Sales (Bar Chart)  
* 🚚 Order Count by Ship Mode  
* 🌍 Delivery Time by Region (Bar Chart)  
* 📉 Discount vs. Profit Scatter Plot  

---

### 📄 Page 3: Time Series Forecasting  

#### • Business Problem  
Without proper forecasting, businesses risk inaccurate demand projections and inefficient stock planning.

#### • Goal  
Analyze growth rates, month-over-month fluctuations, and build sales forecasts using moving averages.

#### • Key Visuals  

* 📊 Profit per Order: \$58.62  
* 📈 Sales Forecast: \$12.6M  
* ⏳ Sales & Profit Growth Year-on-Year  
  - 2011–2014 Growth: 17–34% annually  
* 📆 Month-over-Month Sales Change (%)  
  - Variability tracked from 2011 to early 2015  
* 📈 Cumulative Sales Trends  
  - Performance improvement shown per year

## 🚀 Business Impact & Insights  

* Track regional profit contributors and optimize resource allocation  
* Identify high-value customers and improve targeted campaigns  
* Reduce delivery delays and enhance customer satisfaction  
* Predict demand and align supply chain for seasonal fluctuation
